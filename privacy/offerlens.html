Privacy Policy for OfferLens

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

OfferLens ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. IF YOU DO NOT AGREE WITH THE TERMS OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY, PLEASE DO NOT ACCESS THE APPLICATION.

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Data

When you register for the App (e.g., through anonymous sign-in or potential future account creation), we may collect information that identifies you, such as:

  • Device Identifiers: Unique ID for your mobile device.
  • User Preferences: Selected banks and payment types to personalize offer discovery.

B. Usage Data

We automatically collect information when you access the App:

  • Log Information: Details about how you use the App, including offer clicks, features accessed, and interaction times.
  • Financial Interaction: Information about which offers you view and save (stored locally or in secure cloud storage).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information collected about you via the App to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Personalize your experience by showing offers matching your selected banks/cards.
  • Provide and maintain the Savings Calculator functionality.
  • Deliver interstitial and banner advertisements (for non-premium users).
  • Perform analytics to improve App performance and user experience.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information in certain situations:

  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors (e.g., Google Firebase for authentication/database, Google AdMob for ads).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Transformation: We aggregate data into anonymized analytics that do not identify individual users.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Google Analytics for Firebase

5. Security & Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • SQLCipher Encryption: Your local data (saved offers, etc.) is encrypted using SQLCipher.
  • Firestore Security Rules: We restrict access to user data ensuring users only interact with their own information.
  • Anonymous Sign-In: We prioritize privacy by allowing users to explore offers without mandatory personal identification.

6. Your Rights (DPDP Act Compliance)

In accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (India), you have the right to:

  • Access your data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Withdraw consent/request data deletion.
  • Opt-out of personalized tracking.

7. Merchant Rights & Opt-Out

OfferLens aggregates publicly available offer information. We respect the intellectual property and business preferences of merchants and banks.

  • Merchant Opt-Out: If you are a merchant whose offers are displayed and wish to have them removed, please use our Merchant Opt-Out Form (available on the platform).
  • Data Accuracy: Merchants can request corrections to offer details via our contact channels.
