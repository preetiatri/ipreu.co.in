PayWise Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

PayWise is designed to give you control over your financial digital life while ensuring your sensitive information stays private. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to a zero-knowledge architecture.

2. Zero-Knowledge Data Storage

Exclusive Local Storage: All information regarding your subscriptions, payment dates, and costs is stored locally on your device's memory. We do not use server-side databases for your financial records.

Encryption: PayWise uses industry-standard encryption to protect your data on-device, ensuring that only you can access your information.

3. Information Collection and Use

No Identity Tracking: We do not require account creation, name, email, or any financial identifiers (like bank account numbers or credit card details) to use PayWise.

On-Device Processing: We may use on-device machine learning to categorize your expenses. This processing happens entirely on your hardware; no financial data is sent to our servers for analysis.

4. Third-Party Services

PayWise does not integrate with third-party tracking or advertising SDKs. We do not share your payment behavior or subscription data with any third party.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is local, you have full control. You can clear your data within the app settings or delete all data by uninstalling the application.

6. Children's Privacy

PayWise is intended for adult users and is not marketed to children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy. Any updates will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
