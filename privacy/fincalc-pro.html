FinCalc Pro Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

FinCalc Pro is built to provide accurate financial calculations without compromising your privacy. This policy explains our offline-first approach to financial utility.

2. Data Handling

Offline Calculations: All calculations, including loan amortizations, SIP projections, and tax estimations, are performed locally on your device. No financial data ever leaves your device.

No Storage of Sensitive Values: FinCalc Pro does not store the specific values you input unless you explicitly save a calculation profile, which is stored securely in your device's private app data.

3. Information We Collect

SecurePassword does not collect any personally identifiable information. We do not require account creation or link your calculations to any identity.

4. Third-Party Services

FinCalc Pro does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services. Your financial inquiries are your business alone.

5. Data Deletion

Uninstalling the app will remove any saved profiles and all cached data from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

FinCalc Pro is not intended for use by children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

Updates to this policy will be posted here with an updated revision date.
