FinCalc Pro Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

FinCalc Pro provides professional math and financial tools with a strict focus on user privacy. This policy explains our data-free approach to utility tools.

2. No Data Collection

Pure Local Execution: Every calculation you perform in FinCalc Pro—from compound interest to loan amortizations—happens locally on your device in real-time. We do not log, transmit, or store your calculations or the values you input.

Personal Information: We do not collect any personal information, location data, or device identifiers. FinCalc Pro does not require account creation or any user identification.

3. No Ads or Tracking

FinCalc Pro is completely ad-free and contains no third-party tracking scripts or analytics SDKs. We believe your financial planning is private and should remain so.

4. Data Retention

As we do not collect any data, there is no data to retain. Any temporary input data is cleared once the application is closed or the calculation is reset by the user.

5. Security

Since all operations are local and no data is stored on our servers, your privacy is as secure as your device. We recommend maintaining standard security practices on your mobile device.

6. Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
