OfferLens Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

OfferLens is committed to protecting your privacy while helping you find the best value in your shopping. This policy describes our data-light approach to offer discovery.

2. Information Collection

Anonymous Usage: OfferLens does not require an account, email address, or any personal identifying information. You can use all features anonymously.

Device Data: We may access basic device information (e.g., location if you choose to search for local offers) only to provide the service. This data is processed in real-time and not stored on our servers.

3. How We Use Information

Your search queries and location data are used solely to provide relevant shopping offers. We do not build user profiles or track your shopping habits over time.

4. Third-Party Services

OfferLens displays offers from various merchants. Clicking an offer will take you to the merchant's site, which has its own privacy policy. We do not share your private data with these merchants.

5. Data Retention

We do not retain any of your search history or personal preferences on our servers.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
