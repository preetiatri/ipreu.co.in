Privacy Policy for OfferLens

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

OfferLens ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Data

When you register for the App, we may collect information that identifies you, such as:

  • Device Identifiers: Unique ID for your mobile device used for personalization and ad tracking.
  • User Preferences: Selected banks and payment types to personalize offer discovery.

B. Usage Data

We automatically collect information when you access the App:

  • Log Information: Details about how you use the App, including offer clicks, features accessed, and interaction times.
  • Financial Interaction: Information about which offers you view and save (stored locally or in secure cloud storage).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information collected about you via the App to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Personalize your experience by showing offers matching your selected banks/cards.
  • Provide and maintain the Savings Calculator functionality.
  • Deliver advertisements (banners and interstitials) for non-premium users.
  • Perform analytics to improve App performance and user experience.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Google Firebase (authentication/database) and Google AdMob (advertisements).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics

5. Security & Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • SQLCipher Encryption: Your local data is encrypted.
  • Firestore Security Rules: We restrict access to personal data.

6. Merchant Rights & Opt-Out

OfferLens aggregates publicly available offer information. We respect the preferences of merchants and banks.

  • Merchant Opt-Out: If you are a merchant and wish to have your offers removed, please use our Merchant Opt-Out Form.
