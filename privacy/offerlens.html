OfferLens Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

OfferLens is built to provide high-precision shopping analytics while respecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information and our commitment to transparency.

2. Information Collection and Use

No Personally Identifiable Information: We do not require account registration, name, email address, or phone number to use OfferLens.

Locally Processed Data: All deal analysis and cashback calculations are performed directly on your device. We use locally-scoped logic to determine the best offers without uploading your browsing history to our servers.

3. Third-Party Links and Merchants

Our application contains links to merchant websites (e.g., Amazon, Flipkart). Once you click these links and leave the OfferLens environment, you are subject to the privacy policies of those respective merchants. We do not control or take responsibility for their privacy practices.

4. Data Security

Since OfferLens does not store your data on our servers, the primary security responsibility lies with protecting your own device. We recommend using standard device security features (PIN, Pattern, Biometrics).

5. Data Retention

Any settings or preferences you save within the app are stored locally on your device and can be cleared by deleting the app's cache or uninstalling the app.

6. Children's Privacy

OfferLens is not marketed towards or intended for use by children under 13 years of age.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at support@ipreu.co.in.
