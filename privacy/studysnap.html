StudySnap Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

StudySnap is an AI-powered educational assistant developed as part of the IPREU suite. This Privacy Policy explains our dual-layer approach to data processing, combining local power with global intelligence.

2. Information Processing Layers

Local OCR Layer: Text extraction from your photos and PDFs happens directly on your device using on-device vision libraries. This data is processed in real-time and remains under your control.

AI Interaction Layer: When you ask questions, queries are sent to the Google Gemini API to generate helpful responses. This data is used solely for generating responses and is not retained on IPREU servers.

3. Information Collection

Account-Free Usage: StudySnap does not require account creation, email addresses, or any personal identifying information.

Local History: Your chat history and processed documents are stored locally on your device for your convenience and are not synced to our servers.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We leverage industry-leading services to provide StudySnap's intelligence:

  • Google Gemini API: For natural language processing and question answering.
  • Google ML Kit: For high-performance on-device text recognition.

Data sent to these services is governed by their respective privacy policies. We recommend reviewing Google's Privacy Policy for more detail.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You have total control over your academic data. You can clear your chat history or delete processed files within the app settings. Deleting the app will remove all local data associated with StudySnap.

6. Children's Privacy

StudySnap is designed for students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate parental consent context as defined by applicable laws.

7. Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this policy will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact

For any questions regarding your privacy while using StudySnap, please reach out to us at support@ipreu.co.in.
